Do not miss the opportunity to meet other Renku Users and learn more how they leverage Renku for data science, for managing research and for teaching. Moreover, we will share Renku 2.0 status and the features prioritised for the second half of 2024.

On June 27th between 13:00pm CET and 17.30pm CET, the SDSC team developing Renku invites you to a hybrid Meeting hold both in Bern and via Zoom. 

[Register here !](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfI-dYL09s_A2RQkNikHT7-mSB63KgMQ_T5lm6k9lAtL1mS9A/viewform?usp=pp_url)

13.00-13.30h Welcome and Introduction
